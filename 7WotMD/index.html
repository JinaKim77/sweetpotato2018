The Seven Wonders of the Modern World

Introduction

Wonders of the World are the most spectacular natural or man-made creations. There exist numerous lists that include different wonders, from different time periods of our world, and in most cases those lists cap at seven. This is because the Greeks believed number seven represented perfection.

Other Wonders

As mentioned above, there is a lot more world wonders out there than just 7. These are included in other lists, such as Seven Wonders of the Ancient World or the Seven Wonders of the Middle Ages. Some representatives of these lists are:

  • Statue of Zeus at Olympia
  • Mausoleum at Halicarnassus
  • Lighthouse of Alexandria
  • Hanging Gardens of Babylon
  • Colossus of Rhodes
  • Great Pyramid of Giza
  • Stonehenge
  • Catacombs of Kom el Shoqafa
  • Porcelain Tower of Nanjing
  • Hagia Sophia

The Seven Wonders of the Modern World

Despite there being so many wonderful places and creations to talk about, I will focus on the wonders of our modern world. The list is composed of 7 monuments that were chosen from among 200 candidates in 2007 in Lisbon and also include an honorary 8th member, The Great Pyramid of Giza, as it's the only original/ancient wonder still standing to this day.
